MS in Computer Science in USA Admissions

MS in computer science from USA is one of the popular courses, thus there are certain qualification criteria for getting admitted to the top USA universities. There are proficiency tests of the international level that are needed to be cleared. These tests likewise incorporate English Language Proficiency (for example IELTS, TOEFL) and GRE.

Required Documents

1] Complete Admission Form

2] Payment Receipt of the admission form

3] Academic Transcripts

4] Scores of standardized exams to study in USA, like IELTS, TOEFL, etc.

5] Degree Certificates

6] Copy of Passport

7] Letter of recommendation
